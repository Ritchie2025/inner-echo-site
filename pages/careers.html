Working at Inner Echo

At Inner Echo, we're dedicated to creating safe, judgment-free spaces where people can express themselves fully and experience the profound relief that comes from being truly heard.

Our team members share a deep commitment to emotional wellbeing, authentic connection, and the transformative power of listening. We believe that everyone deserves a space to be heard without judgment, advice, or the pressure to change.

As we grow, we're looking for compassionate, present individuals who resonate with our mission and want to make a meaningful difference in people's lives through the simple yet profound act of listening.

Our Core Values

Compassionate Presence

We believe in the healing power of undivided attention and emotional attunement. Our team members cultivate the ability to be fully present with others, offering their complete attention without distraction.

Radical Acceptance

We welcome all emotions and experiences without judgment, advice, or the pressure to change. Our team members practice non-judgment and create spaces where clients feel safe to express themselves authentically.

Continuous Growth

We're committed to ongoing personal and professional development. Our team members engage in regular reflection, skill-building, and deepening their capacity for empathetic listening.

Current Openings

Listening Specialist

Part-time / Remote

Role Overview

As a Listening Specialist, you'll provide one-on-one listening sessions to clients seeking a safe space to express themselves. You'll create a judgment-free environment where clients can process their thoughts and feelings through the simple yet profound act of being heard.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct virtual listening sessions with clients
  • Create a safe, non-judgmental space for emotional expression
  • Practice deep, attentive listening without offering advice or direction
  • Maintain appropriate boundaries and confidentiality
  • Participate in regular supervision and professional development

Qualifications

  • Background in counseling, psychology, social work, or related field preferred
  • Training in active listening techniques
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • High emotional intelligence and empathy
  • Ability to maintain presence and attention during sessions
  • Commitment to personal growth and self-awareness

Hours & Compensation

This position offers flexible scheduling with a minimum commitment of 10 hours per week. Compensation is competitive and based on experience.

Client Experience Coordinator

Part-time / Remote

Role Overview

As the Client Experience Coordinator, you'll be the first point of contact for clients and ensure a seamless, supportive experience from initial inquiry through session completion. You'll help maintain the warm, welcoming atmosphere that defines Inner Echo.

Key Responsibilities

  • Respond to client inquiries and provide information about services
  • Assist with scheduling and booking management
  • Support clients with technical issues or questions
  • Gather and respond to client feedback
  • Maintain client records and ensure data privacy

Qualifications

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong organizational abilities and attention to detail
  • Experience in customer service or client relations
  • Empathetic approach to client interactions
  • Proficiency with scheduling software and basic technology
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and professional boundaries

Hours & Compensation

This position requires 15-20 hours per week with some flexibility in scheduling. Compensation is competitive and based on experience.
