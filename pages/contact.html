Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions below, or visit our complete FAQ page for more detailed information.

What happens during a listening session?

During a session, you'll have a dedicated space to express whatever is on your mind without interruption or judgment. I'll listen attentively, occasionally reflecting back what I'm hearing to help deepen your awareness. The focus is entirely on creating space for you to process your thoughts and feelings through the act of expressing them.

How do I book a session?

To book a session, simply click the "Book Your Safe Space" button at the top of any page. You'll need to create an account or log in, then select your preferred service and available time slot. After completing your booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with all the details you need for your session.
